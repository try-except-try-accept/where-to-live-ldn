City of Lincoln Council Elections

City of Lincoln Council Elections – Thursday 7 May 2026

Elections were held for the City of Lincoln Council on 7 May 2026.

11 seats, one from each of the 11 wards in the city, were up for election.

Guidance for Candidates and Agents can be found on the Electoral Commission website.
